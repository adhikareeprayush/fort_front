FORCE OF RAPID
TRANSFORMATION (FORT)

FORTs (Force of Rapid Transformation) are full-scale incubators established by SIPCOT under the aegis of Industries Department of Govt of Tamil Nadu in partnership with Forge (Strategic Partner). These centres are set in the heart of industrial parks in Chennai (Sriperumbudur) and Hosur, aimed at catalysing industrial transformation in the digital age - combining manufacturing excellence with technology leadership, entrepreneurial innovation, and future-ready talent.
