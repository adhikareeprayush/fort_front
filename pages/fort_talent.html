BUILDING INNOVATION-CAPABLE INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE & ENTREPRENEURIAL TALENT

A comprehensive and unique portfolio of University Accredited/Affiliated graduate education programs and professional development courses in Innovation & Entrepreneurship.

Innovation Practicum

Talent Creation for Research, Entrepreneurship & Industry

The Innovation Practicum designed and developed by Forge redefines what it means to be an engineer, by equipping the Future and Present workforces, i.e., students and industrial workers, with the potent blend of technology, innovation, and entrepreneurial spirit needed to tackle industry challenges head-on.

TALENT CREATION
IN TECHNICAL EDUCATION

Forge's accredited innovation and entrepreneurship programs seamlessly integrate into existing curricula, enabling both educators and students to foster a culture of creativity and critical thinking - ultimately transforming aspiring engineers into Innovation Engineers: problem-solvers unbound by traditional limitations. Through interdisciplinary collaboration and hands-on prototyping, these programs enable students to develop & deploy Minimum Usable Prototypes – real-world solutions for real-world problems.

MASTERS IN
INNOVATION MANAGEMENT

Forge's Masters in Technology Management (MTM) for industry professionals and Masters in Business Administration - Innovation, Entrepreneurship & Venture Development (MBA-IEV) are AICTE-approved Post-Graduate/Masters programs dedicated to fostering technology-driven, innovation-led entrepreneurship via hands-on, experiential Program to transform Innovators into Entrepreneurs. This transformative journey equips aspiring innovators with essential skills and frameworks to move systematically from concept to commercialisation, leveraging Forge's state-of-the-art industrial innovation labs and resident expertise & resources to enable participants to design, develop, demo & deploy industrial-grade process and product innovations.

TALENT ENRICHMENT
IN INDUSTRY (IP-IW)

Forge helps industrial companies upgrade their present skilled workforce, from operators to executives, into future-ready innovative talent, with practical tools and mindset of Innovation & Technology Management. IP-IW (Innovation Practicum for Industrial Workforce) refreshes and empowers the industrial workforce, unlocking their potential to solve real-world problems and propel the industry to global competitiveness. This initiative bridges the gap between academic theory and on-the-ground application through the robust Practicum training model, ensuring industrial companies harness the full potential of the innovative workforce.

ENTREPRENEURIAL RESEARCH
TALENT IN ACADEMIA (IP-AR)

Academia brims with untapped talent and R&D prowess but doesn't produce many breakthrough innovations. Forge bridges the gap to channel the powerhouse potential of Academia into real-world solutions for solving the industry's toughest technology challenges. IP-AR [Innovation Practicum for Academic Research] accelerates the translation of scientific research into tangible innovation projects that emerge as dominant technologies, creating new markets and dominating existing markets.
