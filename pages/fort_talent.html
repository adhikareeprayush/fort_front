DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS

Amid the accelerated shift in technology's role in manufacturing as the only source of enduring competitive advantage for industrial companies, FORTs enable manufacturing enterprises (small-medium-large) in the upgrade of their workforce - from operators, engineers, managers & executives, into innovation-capable techno-managerial and executive management - a crucial factor for industrial enterprises to emerge as globally competitive tech companies in the manufacturing business.

TECHNOLOGY PRACTICUM FOR
INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE

Manufacturing Technology Practicum

The Manufacturing Technology Practicum (MTP) is a comprehensive capability pathway that advances professionals from digital literacy to enterprise leadership in intelligent manufacturing. Blending classroom instruction, advanced labs, and industry-embedded projects, the program ensures applied learning at every stage. Its curriculum spans Digital Manufacturing Fundamentals, Automation & Data Analytics, and Advanced Production Technologies, enabling a coherent progression of skills. Participants gain expertise in Cyber-Physical Systems, Industrial IoT, Digital Twins, AI/ML, Advanced Robotics, Predictive Analytics, and Additive Manufacturing. Specialised modules on Digital Twin implementation and Autonomous Mobility Robotics prepare learners for the next frontier of smart factories. By aligning workforce development with the imperatives of ADPIM, MTP creates future-ready leaders who drive competitiveness, sustainability, and innovation at scale.
