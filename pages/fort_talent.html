INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE
DEVELOPMENT PROGRAMS

Amid the accelerated shift in technology's role in manufacturing as the only source of enduring competitive advantage for industrial companies, FORTs enable manufacturing enterprises (small-medium-large) in the upgrade of their workforce - from operators, engineers, managers & executives, into innovation-capable techno-managerial and executive management - a crucial factor for industrial enterprises to emerge as globally competitive tech companies in the manufacturing business.

1

TECHNOLOGY PRACTICUM
FOR INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE

Enabling Industrial Workforces to harness technological capabilities across key Industrial Technology Thrust Areas (ITTAs) - advancing from User/Operator-level proficiency with basic tech awareness, limited hands-on exposure, and supervised tasks - to Engineer-level proficiency, defined by deep contextual understanding & system development capabilities resulting in MVP design, development & delivery.

2

INNOVATION PRACTICUM
FOR INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE

Building Innovator-grade talent across Industrial Workforces to create innovation-capable techno-managerial leaders who drive Industry-Anchored (User-Led) Innovation Projects, leveraging cutting-edge lab infrastructure and resident expertise at FORTs to accelerate process innovations - also offered as university-accredited Diploma/Degree Programs such as the Masters in Technology Management (MTM) Program.

INNOVATION PRACTICUM FOR
INDUSTRIAL WORKFORCE

TALENT ENRICHMENT IN INDUSTRY

Forge helps industrial companies upgrade their present skilled workforce, from operators to executives, into future-ready innovative talent, with practical tools and mindset of Innovation & Technology Management. IP-IW (Innovation Practicum for Industrial Workforce) refreshes and empowers the industrial workforce, unlocking their potential to solve real-world problems and propel the industry to global competitiveness. This initiative bridges the gap between academic theory and on-the-ground application through the robust Practicum training model, ensuring industrial companies harness the full potential of the innovative workforce.

ACADEMIC TECHNICAL
EDUCATION PROGRAMS

FORTs deliver Forge’s Technology & Innovation Practicum that offer accredited programs in technology, innovation and entrepreneurship, seamlessly integrating into existing academic curricula. Designed to foster a culture of creativity, product mindset, critical thinking and solution-oriented thinking, these programs transform aspiring engineers into Innovation Engineers — individuals equipped to solve complex, real-world problems through interdisciplinary collaboration and hands-on prototyping.

1

TECHNOLOGY PRACTICUM
FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION

Enabling Educators and Engineers in Academia to harness technological capabilities across key Industrial Technology Thrust Areas (ITTAs)—progressing from User-level skills gained through hands-on learning to Engineer-level proficiency with deep systems understanding to manage complexity and drive the development of advanced technology solutions.

2

INNOVATION PRACTICUM
FOR TECHNICAL EDUCATION

Building Innovator-grade talent across educators & students in Academia that can ultimately drive Academia-anchored (Faculty/Student-led) Innovation projects to co-create frugal, deployable process innovations and marketable products with industrial companies, public sector departments, and tech startups.
