Forge's CVS Compass is a unified strategic planning framework for maximising value through Corporate-Startup engagements - offering an all encompassing visualisation tool to plan, execute, manage and govern strategic corporate innovation through corporate venturing programs. The Compass leverages a STRATEGY SANDBOX at its core, which serves as the contextual lens to implement VALUE CREATION PROGRAMS (Startup Engagement Programs). These programs are selected, scoped and sequenced for implementation are tied to VALUE CAPTURE MODELS (Startup Partnership Models) for unlocking financial, commercial, organisational, and operational value. Vedanta Spark is a groundbreaking corporate open innovation initiative aimed at transforming the energy and resources sector through collaboration with startups. In partnership with Forge Innovation & Ventures, the program explores 16 opportunity areas to drive digital transformation and sustainability.

It offers two engagement models: commercial partnerships, which involve paid pilot projects that can scale into comprehensive solutions, and equity investments that capture technological advantages. Startups retain their intellectual property while gaining access to Vedanta's extensive ecosystem. With 80 startups engaged across 120 projects, Vedanta Spark exemplifies a commitment to operational excellence and sustainable innovation in India's industrial landscape.

Airbus' Innovation Scouting & Industrialisation program, a platform under Airbus Scale's Open Innovation vertical, aims to accelerate joint-industrialisation projects by connecting Indian startups with Airbus business units globally. The initiative focuses on partnering with innovative startups in aerospace, defence, AI, unmanned systems, industrial automation, sustainable aviation, connectivity, autonomy, and space technologies.

Selected startups receive unique opportunities, including collaboration with Airbus domain experts, strategic advisory, market access across Airbus business units, and a Proof of Concept fund for product validation. The program enables startups to develop scale-ready products with compelling business cases, supporting Airbus' 'local to global' strategic approach.
